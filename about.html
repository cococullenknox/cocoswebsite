Background

As an Environmental Sociologist, I focus on bridging the gap between scientific research and practical implementation through innovative stakeholder engagement and knowledge translation strategies.

With expertise in marine conservation and environmental governance, I work to facilitate meaningful dialogue between researchers, policymakers, and communities to drive sustainable environmental solutions.

My Approach

Research

Conducting thorough analysis of environmental challenges through a sociological lens, considering both scientific and social implications.

Engage

Facilitating meaningful dialogue between stakeholders to ensure diverse perspectives are considered in environmental decision-making.

Impact

Developing practical solutions that bridge scientific research with community needs and policy requirements.
