Tasmanian Seafood Industry Assessment

2023 Tasmanian Seafood Industry

Overview

Comprehensive stakeholder analysis and policy recommendations for sustainable industry practices in Tasmania's seafood sector.

Challenge

The Tasmanian seafood industry faced challenges in balancing economic growth with sustainable practices and stakeholder interests.

Approach

  • Conducted extensive stakeholder interviews across the industry
  • Analyzed current policies and practices
  • Developed recommendations for sustainable growth
  • Facilitated industry workshops for feedback and refinement

Outcome

Delivered a comprehensive report with actionable recommendations that were adopted by industry leaders, leading to improved sustainability practices and stakeholder engagement.
